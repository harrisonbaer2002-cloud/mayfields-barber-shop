The Shop

Barbering the way it used to be

Step inside and the clock winds back. A pressed-tin ceiling, a green-and-cream checkerboard floor, walls covered in '50s and '60s memorabilia, and an old Coca-Cola machine standing in the corner.

Doug Mayfield treats everyone in his chair the same — whether you're retired military or coming straight off a job site. You'll get a clean, no-frills cut, an honest price, and the kind of conversation that's gotten harder to find.

It's a Main Street fixture in downtown Simpsonville, an easy walk from the shops and restaurants nearby.
